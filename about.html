About us

Welcome to our Pet Shop, your premier destination for all things pet-related. Located in Balulang, Cagayan de Oro, we are dedicated to providing exceptional care and products for your beloved pets. With a passion for animals and years of experience, we strive to create a warm and inviting environment where both pets and their owners feel welcome. Our team of trained professionals offers a wide range of services, including expert grooming, comfortable pet boarding, professional training, and comprehensive veterinary care. We take pride in curating a diverse selection of top-quality pet products, including nutritious food, engaging toys, stylish accessories, and cozy bedding, ensuring that your pets have everything they need to live happy and healthy lives. At our Pet Shop, we are not just a Pet shop; we are a community of pet lovers who are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and supporting pet adoption and rescue organizations. Join us in our mission to provide exceptional care, expert advice, and endless love to all pets that walk through our doors.
